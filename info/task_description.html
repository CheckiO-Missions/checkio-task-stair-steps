There is a staircase with N steps and two platforms - one at the beginning, other at the end of the stairs. On every step some number is written (from -100 to 100 , with the exception of 0). Zeros are written on the platforms. You start up the stairs from the first platform and finish on the last one. You can move either to the next step or to the next plus one. Find the best path to maximize the sum of numbers on the stairs on your way up and return the final sum.

Input: Numbers on the stairs. A list of integers.

Output: The final sum for the best way. An integer.

Example: 

checkio([5, -3, -1, 2]) == 6
checkio([5, 6, -10, -7, 4]) == 8
checkio([-11, 69, 77, -51, 23, 67, 35, 27, -25, 95]) == 393
checkio([-21, -23, -69, -67, 1, 41, 97, 49, 27]) == 125
